[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Your shadow dancing softly on the hardwood floor
I trace the way you move and leave me wanting more
The coffee’s going cold but I’m burning inside
Just one more touch before you slip into the night

[Verse 1]
The midnight moon is catching on your silver ring
I lean in closer just to hear the way you breathe
We haven't spoken much but there is so much said
Your fingers running slowly through my tangled head

[Pre-Chorus]
[Echoing] (Every time you pull away, I pull you near)
This tender pull is all I ever want to hear
The engine of my truck can wait another hour
I’m caught inside the magic of your quiet power

[Chorus]
I’m tasting your goodbye upon my open lips
A slow-burn fire starting at my fingertips
You’re half out the door but you’re still looking back
My heart is racing down a one-way dirt track

[Post-Chorus]
[Smooth vocal run] (Oh, you're looking back, you're looking back)
Don't let this moment fade to black

[Verse 2]
The scent of vanilla lingering on your coat
The words I want to say are catching in my throat
Your eyes are a mirror of the things we hide
A sudden rush of heat I cannot keep inside

[Pre-Chorus 2]
[Echoing] (Every time you pull away, I pull you near)
The world outside is fading, it is just us here
The engine of my truck can wait another hour
I’m caught inside the magic of your quiet power

[Chorus]
I’m tasting your goodbye upon my open lips
A slow-burn fire starting at my fingertips
You’re half out the door but you’re still looking back
My heart is racing down a one-way dirt track

[Post-Chorus 2]
[Smooth vocal run] (Oh, you're looking back, you're looking back)
Don't let this moment fade to black

[Bridge]
Your shadow dancing softly on the hardwood floor
I trace the way you move and leave me wanting more
The shadows are long and the hour is late
I want you to stay till the morning light breaks
I need you to know what this feeling awakes
Just lay in these arms and surrender to Saeed

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (I don't want you to go just yet)
[Spoken] (Let the world wait outside)

[Final Chorus]
I’m tasting your goodbye upon my open lips
A slow-burn fire starting at my fingertips
You’re half out the door but you’re still looking back
My heart is racing down a one-way dirt track
